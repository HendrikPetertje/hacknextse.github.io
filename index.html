what

a cabin-hack in the middle of Sweden

since 2014 a small self-organizing hack have taken place in Järvsö, groups of friends have meet to hack on hobby projects, socialize and eat well!

dates

2019-11-21

to

2019-11-24

location

cabins on the mountainside of järvsö

how

  1. gather your best hacker and designer friends
  2. book a cabin around alpstigen through jarvso.se
  3. book a minibus/train/car to Järvsö
  4. pack your stuff
  5. profit!

If you book a cabin, reach out to me on twitter.

info

  • Most cabins have a power budget of 150W/person
  • Most cabins have a wifi
  • You can cook in the cabins or eat out in Järvsö
  • Tip: You can have cleaning included in your booking
