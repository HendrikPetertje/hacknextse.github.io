since 2014 a small group of developers have gotten together in cabin in Järvsö to hack on hobby projects, and eat and drink well — now we want more hackers!

why

The real benefits of being far away from home is that you are commited to what you are doing and that is good for your inner coding-zen. No chores or duties can disturb your focus in the middle of the mountains unless you decide so. We tend to focus on networking and honing our skills as developers.

how

  1. gather your best hacker and designer friends
  2. book a cabin around alpstigen through jarvso.se
  3. book a mini bus/train/car to järvsö
  4. pack your stuff
  5. profit!

  • phone & charger

info

  • The event is free, but you pay for all your expenses and organize yourself
  • Most cabins have a power budget of 150W/person
  • Most cabins have a wifi, bring personal 4G if speedfreak
  • Cross-cabin joint hotel dinner on saturday
  • You can cook in the cabins or eat out in Järvsö
  • You are responsible for your own cabin and your own food
  • Tip: You can have cleaning included in your booking
